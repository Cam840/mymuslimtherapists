Dr. Fatima Hassan

Dr. Fatima is a licensed psychologist with over 15 years of experience supporting Muslim communities through culturally sensitive, evidence-based mental health care. As the daughter of immigrants and a practicing Muslim herself, she deeply understands the intersection of faith, family, and emotional wellbeing. She founded mymuslimtherapists to create a space where clients never have to explain their identity before beginning their healing journey.

What We Offer

Therapy services in Johns Creek

mymuslimtherapists offers a full range of counseling services designed to meet your unique mental health needs — while honoring your faith, culture, and identity. We provide care in person and online.

Individual Therapy

One-on-one sessions with a therapist who provides care personalized to your needs — whether you're managing a mental health condition, navigating a life change, or seeking personal growth.

Learn More →
Couples Therapy

Build a stronger marriage or partnership through guided sessions. Whether pre-marital counseling, navigating conflict, or rebuilding trust, we're here for every stage.

Learn More →
Online Therapy

Access high-quality mental health care from anywhere with a secure internet connection. Ideal for busy schedules, remote clients, or those preferring the comfort of home.

Learn More →
Family Therapy

Strengthen family bonds and resolve conflict with a skilled facilitator. Bring in any family members — partners, children, parents, or extended family.

Learn More →
Play Therapy

Children communicate through play. Our play therapists help younger children process emotions, trauma, and behavioral challenges in a safe, expressive environment.

Learn More →
Grief Therapy

Loss — whether a loved one, a marriage, or a way of life — deserves compassionate support. We help you process grief and find a path forward with meaning and hope.

Learn More →
Trauma-Informed Therapy

Our therapists approach every client with an awareness of how trauma — including religious, racial, and intergenerational — shapes your wellbeing and sense of self.

Learn More →
Group Therapy

Heal in community with others who share your experiences. Our culturally-focused groups are designed for Muslims navigating shared challenges in today's world.

A closed support group for Muslim women navigating marriage, motherhood, grief, and personal growth. A space to speak freely, be witnessed, and heal together.
